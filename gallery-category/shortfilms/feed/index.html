Christchurch, Canterbury New Zealand has been through some tough times of late. But now is on the up and up with a fresh new start. Buildings are going up, businesses are opening their doors and tourists are visiting en masse. Flashworks Media filmed this piece in light of the recent quakes hitting NZ.
While the tough times are now for many, there are better times ahead as Christchurch can testify.
Kia Kaha New Zealand!
